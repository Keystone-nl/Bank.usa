Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most.

How do I open an account?

Select "Open Account" from the homepage, fill in your details, and submit — most applications are reviewed within minutes.

How long do transfers take?

Internal transfers are instant. External transfers typically settle within 1–3 business days.

Is my money insured?

Eligible deposits are protected up to the applicable coverage limit. See our Security page for full details.

How do I freeze a lost card?

Sign in, go to your dashboard's card controls, and freeze it instantly — no phone call required.
